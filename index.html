Pricing model rationale — why this structure
Entry motion
Two Detect plans anchor the buy. Simple procurement, familiar SaaS motion, 14-day trial supported.
ADAR non-linear
Modules added in any order. Customer who wants Resolve before Anticipate can do that. No forced ladder.
NRR engine
CS team has a clear capability journey to take customers along. Upsell built into each ADAR phase.
Scale motion
Consumption pricing on users, assets, polygons and API grows revenue automatically with customer footprint.
